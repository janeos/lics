Our Facilities

At Legacy of Grace International Christian School, we provide state-of-the-art facilities to support our students' learning and development.

Science Labs

Dedicated science laboratories equipped with modern equipment for hands-on learning and experiments.

ICT & Robotics Lab

Information and Communication Technology labs with modern computers, software, and robotics equipment for digital learning and programming education.

PBL Room

Project-Based Learning space for hands-on activities, encouraging creativity, problem-solving, and collaborative learning.

Sports Facilities

Indoor sports hall and multipurpose hall for physical education and various sports activities.

Arts Facilities

Dedicated spaces for art, music, and drama, nurturing creative expression and talent.

Libraries

Well-stocked libraries providing resources for research, reading, and academic support.
