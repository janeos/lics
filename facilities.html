Our Facilities

At Legacy of Grace International Christian School, we provide state-of-the-art facilities to support our students' learning and development.

Science Labs

Dedicated science laboratories equipped with modern equipment for hands-on learning and experiments.

ICT Labs

Information and Communication Technology labs with modern computers and software for digital learning.

Interactive Classrooms

Classrooms equipped with interactive Promethean Boards for engaging and interactive learning experiences.

Robotics Lab

Specialized lab for robotics and programming education, fostering innovation and technical skills.

Makers Room

Space for design thinking and project-based learning, encouraging creativity and problem-solving.

Sports Facilities

Indoor sports hall and multipurpose hall for physical education and various sports activities.

Libraries

Well-stocked libraries providing resources for research, reading, and academic support.

Arts Facilities

Dedicated spaces for art, music, and drama, nurturing creative expression and talent.

Science Art Lab

Specialized laboratory combining science and art for innovative learning experiences.
