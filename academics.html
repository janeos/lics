Our Academic Programs

At Legacy of Grace International Christian School, we believe in providing a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in all areas of life. Our academic programs are designed to challenge and inspire students to reach their full potential.

Literacy

Our literacy program is designed to help students develop strong reading and writing skills that will serve them well throughout their academic and professional careers.

Science

We offer a comprehensive science program covering biology, chemistry, physics, and earth science with hands-on experiments and engaging activities.

Chinese

Immerse yourself in Chinese culture and language with our comprehensive Mandarin program, covering phonetics, daily expressions, and cultural understanding.

Writing

Our writing program focuses on building strong foundational skills while encouraging creativity and self-expression.

Geography

We provide a comprehensive understanding of the earth's physical features and human activities, essential for global citizenship.

Thai

Students learn reading, writing, and speaking Thai language, along with Thai culture, traditions, and values.

Mathematics

Our comprehensive math program covers everything from basic arithmetic to advanced calculus, building strong problem-solving skills.

Physical Education

Our program includes various sports and activities designed to develop strength, coordination, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

ICT

Students learn Computer Science including Blender, Scratch & Robotics (mBots), using modern teaching methods and technology.
