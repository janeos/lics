What is the ACE Program?

The Accelerated Christian Education (ACE) program is a comprehensive, Bible-based educational system that has been successfully implemented in over 140 countries worldwide.

What is a PACE?

A PACE (Packet of Accelerated Christian Education) is a self-instructional workbook that contains a complete unit of work. Each PACE:

  • Is designed to be completed in 3-4 weeks
  • Contains clear objectives and instructions
  • Includes self-tests and final tests
  • Provides immediate feedback
  • Allows students to work at their own pace

Key Features of the ACE Program

  • Individualized learning approach
  • Mastery-based progression
  • Biblical integration in all subjects
  • Character development focus
  • Regular diagnostic testing
  • Comprehensive academic validation

Individualized Learning

Students work at their own pace, mastering each concept before moving forward, ensuring a solid foundation in all subjects.

Biblical Integration

All subjects are taught from a Christian perspective, helping students develop a biblical worldview while learning academic content.

Character Development

The program emphasizes character building through biblical principles, helping students develop strong moral values and leadership skills.

Academic Excellence

Regular diagnostic testing and mastery-based progression ensure students achieve academic excellence in all subjects.

Global Recognition

With over 55 years of proven success in 140+ countries, the ACE program is recognized worldwide for its quality education.

College Preparation

Students are well-prepared for higher education through rigorous academic training and character development.
