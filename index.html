Welcome to LICS

Our vision is to become a leading Christian institution known for academic rigor, individualized learning, and the cultivation of morally grounded, spiritually strong future leaders. We strive to empower our students to impact the world for Christ, walking in faith, wisdom, and service.

Faith, Love, Grace, Humility, Patience, Forgiveness, Compassion, Intergrity, Servanthood, Peacefulness, Joy, and Selfcontrol are all Christian Traits used in this school

At Legacy of Grace International Christian School, our mission is to provide a Christ-centered, holistic education that fosters academic excellence, spiritual growth, and character development. We are committed to nurturing each student's God-given potential in a caring and inspiring environment, equipping them to lead lives of purpose, compassion, and integrity.- Director's Message
